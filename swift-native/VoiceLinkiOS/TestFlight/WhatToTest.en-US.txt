Build 93 focuses on audio stability and VoiceOver user actions.

What changed:
- iOS room audio relay now uses a smaller initial prebuffer and a larger packet queue to reduce join-time buffering while keeping playback stable.
- VoiceOver actions on iOS people and bot rows are now based on what each row can actually do.
- The current logged-in user row can still show monitoring and audio controls, but no longer exposes Whisper.
- Other people expose Whisper only when they have audio available.
- Text-only bots expose text actions such as profile and direct message without audio controls.
- Audio bots can expose both text actions and audio-related actions.
- iOS room message rows now expose Reply and Direct Message actions to improve message handling with VoiceOver.
- macOS user rows now mirror the same rule that Whisper is hidden for the current user, with explicit VoiceOver actions for direct message, audio controls, and local monitoring.

Please test:
- Join a room on iOS with VoiceOver enabled.
- Focus your own user row and confirm Monitoring or Audio Controls are available, but Whisper is not.
- Focus another person with audio and confirm Whisper and audio actions are available.
- Focus a text-only bot and confirm only text-related actions are offered.
- Focus an audio-capable bot and confirm both text and audio actions are offered.
- Send and receive room messages on iOS, then confirm message rows expose Reply and Direct Message actions.
- Join a room and confirm audio starts with less buffering and no new distortion or clipping.
- On macOS, confirm your own row does not offer Whisper and still provides local monitoring/audio actions.
