Build 84 focuses on room reliability and administrator testing:

- Room chat now requests and displays a larger prior message history when joining.
- Repeated messages are no longer hidden unless they are immediate duplicates.
- Room system messages keep their server timestamps when available.
- Admin or room-management accounts now see the native Admin controls from inside an active room.
- Room audio controls are compact in the room view, with detailed audio settings in the room audio settings sheet.
- The iOS relay player uses a deeper initial buffer to reduce join-time audio stutter.
- Server room history delivery now sends up to 200 messages on join/request.

Please test joining a room as an admin, opening Room Actions > Room Administration, viewing prior user/system messages, sending repeated test messages, and listening for smoother room audio immediately after join.
