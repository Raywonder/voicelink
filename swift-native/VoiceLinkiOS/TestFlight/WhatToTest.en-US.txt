VoiceLink TestFlight Build 35

This build is the current iOS audio and transcript retest build.
The main focus is room audio stability, louder output/input control, and live transcript behavior while joined to a room.

Main things to test:
- Join a room and confirm room audio starts normally.
- Confirm room audio is actually audible on the phone speaker or connected output.
- Speak after joining and confirm your mic comes through.
- Open Settings and Room Controls and verify Mic Level and Master Output now boost cleanly up to 300 percent.
- Check whether room preview hands off cleanly into the joined room.
- Background the app, return, and confirm room audio still works.
- Use Test Sound in Settings > Audio and confirm it plays.
- Open Room Controls and test Mic Level, Master Output, Show Chat, and Mute Media Playback.
- In an active room, speak for a few seconds from another client and confirm Live Transcripts starts filling in on iOS.
- Check Bluetooth or other routed audio if available and confirm the room still stays audible after route changes.
- Check whether the phone still gets unusually hot while joining or while sitting in one room.

Helpful spot checks:
- VoiceOver should still announce the sliders as real sliders.
- Signed-in and guest room joins should both still work.
- Room lists, people lists, and profile info should still refresh correctly.
- Transcript rows should show the speaker name and the transcribed text.
- Guests should not see admin-only controls.

If something breaks, the best report includes:
- device model and iOS version
- build number 35
- server used
- exact steps
- what you expected
- what actually happened
- screenshot, screen recording, or crash report if available

If audio flips from “working” to “absolutely not,” that transition point is the most useful part to report.
