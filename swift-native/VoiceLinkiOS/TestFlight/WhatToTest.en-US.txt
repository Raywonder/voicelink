Build 86 focuses on iOS room controls, microphone status, and sound parity:

- iOS no longer shows whisper, monitor, or per-user audio controls for your own user row.
- Long-pressing your own user row now gives a clear message instead of setting yourself as a whisper target.
- iOS now reports live microphone sample rate, frame buffer size, channel count, codec, and audio engine metadata with room audio state.
- The iOS room audio status now announces when the microphone is active and shows the current capture format.
- iOS now includes the same bundled sound assets as the macOS app so action, room, whisper, and status sounds do not diverge.
- The Windows desktop shortcut on this laptop has been pointed at the runnable WPF build while the WinUI startup crash is isolated.

Please test joining a room from iOS, opening People in Room, focusing your own user row, and confirming whisper and monitor controls are not offered for yourself. Then focus another user and confirm whisper/monitor actions are still available. Also confirm the room audio status reports microphone active with sample rate and buffer details, and that room/action sounds match the macOS app.
