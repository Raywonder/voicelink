VoiceLink TestFlight Build 28

This is a very important build to test.

Primary focus for this build:
- Join a room and confirm audio starts cleanly instead of buffering, chopping, or staying silent.
- Watch device performance while opening the app and joining a room, including heat, battery drain, and general responsiveness.
- Confirm microphone audio works after joining and that preview audio stops cleanly when the room opens.
- Sign in with a normal VoiceLink account and confirm login stays on VoiceLink's internal auth path.
- Join as a guest with a typed name and confirm the room opens correctly.
- Confirm the app still uses the two-tab layout: Main and Settings.
- Confirm the Profile sheet shows signed-in account state for logged-in users and Sign In / Device Pair actions for guests.
- Confirm room activity and people lists update from the current room.
- Open Support from Settings and confirm it goes to VoiceLink internal support first while WHMCS remains unavailable.

Also verify:
- Web frontend links open correctly from iOS.
- Privacy, terms, and support pages load from the public VoiceLink site.
- Guests do not see admin-only controls.
- Returning to the app after backgrounding does not leave it stuck or blank.
- If audio fails, include whether it buffered forever, chopped briefly, or never started at all.
- If the phone gets warm, include how quickly that happened and whether it was only on open or also after joining a room.

Please include in reports:
- device model and iOS version
- build number 28
- server used
- exact steps
- expected result vs actual result
- screenshot or screen recording if available
