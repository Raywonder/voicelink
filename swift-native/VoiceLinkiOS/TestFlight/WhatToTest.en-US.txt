Build 97 focuses on Mastodon sign-in, safer room audio startup, live transcripts, and server bot management.

What changed:
- Mastodon-authenticated sessions refresh rooms after sign-in and avoid silently downgrading a failed signed-in join to guest mode.
- Microphone controls are hidden until the user is signed in and has actually joined a room.
- The iOS audio socket also refuses to publish microphone/audio state unless a signed-in room session is active.
- Room transcript events continue into the Live Transcripts section so spoken room activity can appear as subtitles when the server provides transcripts.
- VoiceLink server bot defaults now prefer Codex/OpenClaw before local fallback models, with existing role boundaries for visitors, users, moderators, and admins.

Please test:
- Sign in with Mastodon, refresh the server list, and join a room.
- Confirm the microphone controls are not visible from a shared room URL, preview, or sign-in screen.
- Confirm microphone controls appear only after signed-in room join.
- Try a bad or expired sign-in session and confirm the app asks for sign-in again instead of joining as guest.
- Join a room where transcripts are enabled and confirm spoken messages appear in Live Transcripts.
- Confirm room messages, people, room details, and audio settings sheets do not disconnect the room.
