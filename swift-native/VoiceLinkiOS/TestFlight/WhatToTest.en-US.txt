Build 86 focuses on iOS room controls, microphone status, sound parity, and authentication follow-up.

What changed:
- iOS no longer shows whisper, monitor, or per-user audio controls for the current user's own row.
- Long-pressing the current user's own row now gives a clear message instead of setting that user as a whisper target.
- iOS now reports microphone sample rate, frame buffer size, channel count, codec, and audio engine metadata with room audio state.
- Room audio status now announces when the microphone is active and shows the current capture format.
- iOS now includes the same bundled sound assets as the macOS app so action, room, whisper, and status sounds stay consistent.

Please test:
- Join a room from iOS, open People in Room, focus your own user row, and confirm whisper and monitor controls are not offered for yourself.
- Focus another user and confirm whisper and monitor actions are still available where permitted.
- Confirm room audio status reports microphone active, including sample rate and buffer details.
- Confirm room, action, whisper, and status sounds match the macOS app.
- Confirm microphone permission is requested only when needed and does not repeatedly prompt after permission is granted.
- Sign in with Mastodon or another supported authentication method and confirm VoiceLink returns to the app after successful authentication.
