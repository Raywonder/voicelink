Build 96 refreshes the iOS beta with the latest room action, profile sheet, and join-time audio stability fixes.

What changed:
- Showing a user's profile on iOS now opens an in-room details sheet instead of switching away from the room.
- VoiceOver actions for people and bot rows stay in the active room when showing profile details, direct messages, monitoring, whisper, or audio controls.
- The user details sheet now shows room state, transmit state, mute/output state, speaking state, bot type, role, authentication provider, and device/client details when available.
- The current signed-in user row still allows monitoring and audio controls, but does not expose Whisper.
- Other people expose Whisper only when they have audio available.
- Text-only bots expose text actions such as profile and direct message without audio controls.
- Audio-capable bots can expose both text and audio-related actions.
- iOS room audio keeps the larger room I/O buffer and relay packet queue tuning from the last beta to reduce join-time buffering while keeping latency low.
- This beta is a refresh build so testers receive the newest packaged fixes instead of remaining on build 95.

Please test:
- Join a room on iOS with VoiceOver enabled.
- Focus your own user row and confirm Monitoring or Audio Controls are available, but Whisper is not.
- Use the View Profile action and confirm the user details sheet opens without leaving the room or disconnecting.
- Open and close audio controls from your own row and another user's row and confirm the room remains active.
- Focus another person with audio and confirm Whisper and audio actions are available.
- Focus a text-only bot and confirm only text-related actions are offered.
- Focus an audio-capable bot and confirm both text and audio actions are offered.
- Join a room and confirm audio starts with less buffering and no new distortion, clipping, or repeated join sounds.
- Leave the details sheet, People in Room sheet, and audio controls and confirm room messages and users remain visible.
