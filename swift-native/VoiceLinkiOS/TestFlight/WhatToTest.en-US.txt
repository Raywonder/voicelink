VoiceLink TestFlight Build 31

This one is still very much about room audio, but with a sharper goal:
does joining a room finally feel steady, predictable, and boring in the best possible way?

Main things to test:
- Join a room and confirm audio starts without crashing, buffering forever, chopping badly, or staying silent.
- Speak after joining and confirm your mic actually comes through.
- If you use preview first, make sure preview audio stops cleanly once the full room opens.
- Background the app, return, and check that room audio comes back normally instead of going quiet, stuck, or odd.
- Open room controls and test Mic Level, Master Output, and Show Chat with VoiceOver if available.
- Use Test Sound in Settings > Audio and confirm it actually plays.
- Sign in with a normal VoiceLink account and make sure the app stays on the VoiceLink auth path.
- Join as a guest with a typed name and confirm that still works properly.
- Confirm the app still uses the simple Main and Settings tab layout.
- Open Profile and confirm signed-in users see account info, while guests still see the guest-friendly actions.

Nice to spot check while you are there:
- room activity and people lists update correctly
- guests do not see admin-only controls
- support, privacy, and terms links still open correctly
- the phone does not get weirdly hot just from opening the app and joining one room

If something breaks, the best report includes:
- device model and iOS version
- build number 31
- server used
- exact steps
- what you expected
- what actually happened
- screenshot, screen recording, or crash report if available

If audio goes from “sounds good” to “absolutely not,” that exact moment is gold.
