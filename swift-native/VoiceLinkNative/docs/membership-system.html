Trust Score System

Every member has a trust score (0-100) that affects their ability to upgrade levels. The trust score is influenced by community behavior.

Excellent (80-100) Good (60-79) Fair (40-59) Poor (1-39) Banned (0)

Trust Score Effects

Trust Level Score Range Can Upgrade? Effects
Excellent 80-100 Yes Full access, priority upgrades
Good 60-79 Yes Normal access
Fair 40-59 No Cannot upgrade until trust improves
Poor 1-39 No May be demoted, limited features
Banned 0 No Cannot link new devices

Complaint System

Complaints from other users affect your trust score:

Severity Trust Penalty Example
Minor -5 points Minor disruption
Moderate -15 points Harassment
Severe -30 points Repeated violations
Critical -50 points Ban-worthy behavior

Paid Tiers (Bonus)

Paid tiers add bonus devices and rooms on top of your free membership level. These are optional and use a "pay what you can" model.

Supporter Tier

  • +7 Bonus Devices
  • +15 Bonus Rooms
  • Supporter badge

Suggested: $5+

Unlimited Tier

  • +97 Bonus Devices (up to 100 total)
  • +90 Bonus Rooms (up to 100 total)
  • Unlimited badge
  • Priority support

Suggested: $20+

Server API Endpoints

Membership Sync

POST /api/sync/membership

Syncs membership level, stats, and trust score with the server.

Trust Sync

POST /api/sync/trust

Syncs trust score and handles pending complaints from the server.

Wallet Sync

POST /api/sync/wallet

Syncs wallet address and balances with the eCrypto network.

Device Sync

POST /api/sync/devices

Syncs linked devices and handles revocations from the server.
