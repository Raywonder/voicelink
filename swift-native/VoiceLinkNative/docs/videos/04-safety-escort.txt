VoiceLink Native v1.0 - Safety & Escort Me Video Script
======================================================

TITLE: Safety Features and Escort Me

DURATION: 2 minutes

---

SCENE 1: Safety First (10 seconds)
NARRATION:
"VoiceLink prioritizes your safety. Let's explore the Escort Me feature."

VISUALS:
- Shield icon
- Safety badge

SCENE 2: What is Escort Me? (20 seconds)
NARRATION:
"Escort Me lets you request a trusted companion to join your voice chat session.
Whether you feel uncomfortable, need help navigating the app, or want moderation support,
an escort can be there for you."

VISUALS:
- Escort Me button in room controls
- Escort joining animation

SCENE 3: Request Reasons (25 seconds)
NARRATION:
"You can request an escort for various reasons:"

VISUALS (show each with icon):
- "Personal Safety - when you feel unsafe or uncomfortable"
- "New User Help - get guidance using VoiceLink"
- "Accessibility Support - assistance for accessibility needs"
- "Moderation - help moderating your room"

SCENE 4: How to Request (30 seconds)
NARRATION:
"Here's how to use Escort Me:
1. Click the Escort Me button in your room
2. Select your reason
3. Choose where to find escorts: Trusted only, Community, or Anyone
4. Wait for a match
5. Your escort joins your room
6. End the session when you're ready"

VISUALS:
- Step-by-step UI walkthrough
- Each screen transition

SCENE 5: Trusted Escorts (20 seconds)
NARRATION:
"Build a list of trusted escorts - friends you know and trust.
When you set 'Trusted Only', only people in your list will be contacted.
Add and manage trusted escorts in your settings."

VISUALS:
- Trusted escorts list
- Add/remove actions
- Priority settings

SCENE 6: Becoming an Escort (20 seconds)
NARRATION:
"Want to help others? Become a community escort.
Requirements:
- Level 2 membership or higher
- Trust score of 70+
- 14 days active
- No complaints"

VISUALS:
- Escort badge
- Requirements checklist
- Approval animation

SCENE 7: Safety Reminder (15 seconds)
NARRATION:
"Your safety matters. Use Escort Me whenever you need support.
Report any concerning behavior immediately."

VISUALS:
- Shield with checkmark
- Report button
- Community guidelines link

---

END CARD:
