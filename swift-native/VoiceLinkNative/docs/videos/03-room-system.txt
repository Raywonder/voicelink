VoiceLink Native v1.0 - Room System Video Script
================================================

TITLE: Creating and Managing Rooms

DURATION: 2-3 minutes

---

SCENE 1: Room Types Overview (20 seconds)
NARRATION:
"VoiceLink has three types of rooms: Guest rooms, Permanent rooms, and OpenLink rooms.
Let's explore each one."

VISUALS:
- Three room type icons
- Quick preview of each

SCENE 2: Guest Rooms (30 seconds)
NARRATION:
"Guest rooms are for users who haven't logged in yet.
They're quick to create but come with limitations:
- Random duration between 10-30 minutes
- Maximum 15 members
- No lock or password features
- Only one room at a time"

VISUALS:
- Guest room card (orange)
- Timer counting down
- 15 person limit indicator
- Lock icon with X

NARRATION:
"Sign in to unlock permanent rooms with full features!"

SCENE 3: Permanent Rooms (45 seconds)
NARRATION:
"Permanent rooms are for logged-in members.
They never expire and come with full features."

VISUALS:
- Permanent room card (green)
- Forever icon
- Feature list appearing

NARRATION:
"The number of permanent rooms you can create depends on:
- Your membership level: Level 1 gets 2, Level 2 gets 5, Level 3 gets 10
- Paid tier bonuses: Supporters get +15, Unlimited gets +90
- Mastodon reputation bonuses: Up to +8 more"

VISUALS:
- Room count calculation animation
- Base + paid + reputation = total

SCENE 4: Room Capacity (30 seconds)
NARRATION:
"Each room has a member capacity that scales with your level:
- Level 1: 2 members per room
- Level 2: 10 members
- Level 3: 50 members
Plus bonuses from paid tiers and Mastodon reputation.
Unlimited tier members can have 1000+ members per room!"

VISUALS:
- Capacity meter filling
- Bonus calculations
- Unlimited capacity icon

SCENE 5: OpenLink Visitor Rooms (25 seconds)
NARRATION:
"OpenLink rooms are special hidden rooms created automatically when someone initiates a call with a visitor.
These rooms exist while the connection is active, then have a 5-minute grace period after the call ends.
They can extend up to 15 minutes total for data collection."

VISUALS:
- OpenLink icon (purple)
- Hidden room indicator
- Timer sequence: Active -> 5 min grace -> Extended

SCENE 6: Device Rotation (30 seconds)
NARRATION:
"When you have multiple devices linked, your rooms can be hosted across different devices.
Choose your rotation mode:
- Random: Different device each time
- Round Robin: Rotate in order
- Preferred: Use your favorite device
- Load Balanced: Distribute based on capacity"

VISUALS:
- Multiple device icons
- Rotation mode selector
- Animation showing room moving between devices

SCENE 7: Server Capacity (15 seconds)
NARRATION:
"Servers start with 50 room capacity and can grow up to 5000 based on your activity, trust score, and reputation."

VISUALS:
- Server capacity bar
- 50 to 5000 scale
- Factors contributing

---

END CARD:
