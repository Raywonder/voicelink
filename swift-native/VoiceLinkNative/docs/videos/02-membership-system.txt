VoiceLink Native v1.0 - Membership System Video Script
=====================================================

TITLE: Understanding the Membership System

DURATION: 2-3 minutes

---

SCENE 1: Introduction (10 seconds)
NARRATION:
"VoiceLink uses a fair, level-based membership system. Let's explore how it works."

VISUALS:
- Membership badge view
- Level indicator

SCENE 2: The Three Levels (45 seconds)

LEVEL 1 - NEWBIE
NARRATION:
"Everyone starts as a Newbie at Level 1. You get 1 device, 2 rooms, and basic features."

VISUALS:
- Newbie badge (gray star)
- 1 device icon
- 2 room icons

LEVEL 2 - REGULAR
NARRATION:
"After 7 days of activity and 5 room hours, you become a Regular at Level 2.
This unlocks 2 devices and 5 rooms."

VISUALS:
- Regular badge (blue star)
- Progress bar filling
- 2 device icons
- 5 room icons

LEVEL 3 - OUTSTANDING
NARRATION:
"Outstanding members at Level 3 have 30 days active and 20 room hours.
They get 3 devices, 10 rooms, and priority support."

VISUALS:
- Outstanding badge (gold star)
- 3 device icons
- 10 room icons
- Priority badge

SCENE 3: Level = Device Tier (20 seconds)
NARRATION:
"Here's the key: Your level IS your device tier.
Level 1 means 1 device. Level 2 means 2 devices. Level 3 means 3 devices.
It's that simple."

VISUALS:
- Side-by-side comparison
- Level number matching device count

SCENE 4: Trust Score (30 seconds)
NARRATION:
"Your trust score ranges from 0 to 100 and affects upgrades.
Excellent trust (80-100) lets you upgrade freely.
Fair or poor trust means you need to improve before upgrading.
Be a good community member!"

VISUALS:
- Trust score meter
- Color-coded badges: Excellent (green), Good (blue), Fair (yellow), Poor (orange), Banned (red)

SCENE 5: Paid Bonuses (25 seconds)
NARRATION:
"Paid tiers are optional and add bonus devices and rooms on top of your free level.
Supporters get +7 devices and +15 rooms.
Unlimited tier gets up to 100 devices and 100 rooms total."

VISUALS:
- Supporter badge
- Unlimited badge
- Bonus numbers animating

SCENE 6: Mastodon Reputation Bonus (20 seconds)
NARRATION:
"Link your Mastodon account for extra bonuses based on your account's reputation.
Veteran accounts with lots of followers get up to +8 bonus rooms and +50 member capacity."

VISUALS:
- Mastodon logo
- Reputation tiers: New, Standard, Active, Established, Veteran
- Bonus numbers

SCENE 7: Summary (15 seconds)
NARRATION:
"Level up by being active, maintain good trust, and optionally support the project for bonuses."

VISUALS:
- All three level badges
- Trust score
- Paid tier badges
