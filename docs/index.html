‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enable 3D audio?

Go to Audio Settings and check "Enable 3D Spatial Audio". Position users by dragging them in the 3D Audio Space panel.

Can I use VoiceLink on my phone?

Yes! iOS users can use Safari or Chrome browsers. Full mobile apps are planned for the future.

How do I connect my Jellyfin server?

Open Media Player, go to Jellyfin tab, click "Add Server" and enter your server details.
