VoiceLink Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 6, 2026

VoiceLink by Devine Creations provides voice chat, messaging, room administration, optional media playback, support workflows, and federation features. This policy explains what data may be collected or processed by the app, by VoiceLink servers, and by enabled third-party services used by a server operator.

Data We Process

  • Account data: login identifier, authentication provider, session state, and role/permission metadata.
  • Room and chat data: room membership, message history, media metadata, attachment metadata, and moderation events.
  • Operational data: device/session identifiers, server logs, diagnostics, crash reports, and abuse-prevention signals.
  • Optional integrations: provider tokens and identity metadata for enabled services (for example WHMCS, Mastodon, or support integrations).
  • Permissions-based data: microphone, camera, notifications, photo/file access, and local media access only when related features are used.

How Data Is Used

  • Deliver voice, chat, and room features.
  • Authenticate users and enforce role-based access.
  • Protect service integrity, detect abuse, and troubleshoot failures.
  • Support licensing, device activation, support routing, and account recovery when configured by the server owner.
  • Render links, media previews, notifications, and accessibility-first controls in supported clients.

Data Sharing and Third Parties

  • VoiceLink may exchange data with third-party service providers only when a server administrator enables that integration.
  • Examples include identity providers, hosting providers, support systems, or media services used to deliver requested features.
  • VoiceLink does not sell personal data.
  • Server operators remain responsible for the third-party services they enable on their own deployments.

Data Control

  • Retention, moderation, and federation settings are controlled by the server administrator.
  • Self-hosted deployments may process and store data on their own infrastructure.
  • App permissions (microphone, camera, photos, notifications) are requested only when related features are used.

App Store Privacy Summary

VoiceLink and enabled third-party partners may collect data from the app to provide account access, voice and chat features, diagnostics, moderation, support, and other enabled integrations. The exact data categories depend on the features used and the server configuration selected by the server operator.
